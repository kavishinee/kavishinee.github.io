What I Did

For this project, I customized the original Feane template to reflect the identity of a South Indian restaurant. I added a personalized welcome message for my professor at the top of the homepage, styled with a glowing neon blue effect for a bold and modern look. I also added a clear link to the original template, displayed at the top of the page on a warm yellow background to make it noticeable but not distracting. I changed the website’s typography to use Georgia, giving it a more elegant and traditional style to match the theme. Using JavaScript, I created an interactive section at the top of the page where users can input their favorite Indian food and receive a custom response. I also added images to enhance the visual appeal and better showcase South Indian culture. Finally, I included my student ID and name—24229059 – Shangar Kavishinee—in the footer of every page as a clear identifier.

Sambar Specials

This hearty, tangy lentil stew is simmered slowly with tamarind, mustard seeds, curry leaves, and a unique blend of spices, creating a comforting and flavorful experience. Perfectly paired with steamed rice, idlis, or dosas, our sambar is a wholesome dish that’s both nourishing and satisfying. Whether you enjoy it mild or with an extra kick of spice, our sambar will transport your taste buds straight to the kitchens of South India.

Pongal Plates

A beloved breakfast classic made from creamy, slow-cooked rice and yellow moong dal, seasoned with black pepper, ginger, cumin, and curry leaves. This mildly spiced, savory dish is both soothing and satisfying, often served with coconut chutney and tangy sambar on the side. Perfect for starting your day with authentic taste and warmth, our Pongal Plates bring tradition and simplicity together in every delicious bite.

Biryani Bliss

A fragrant, flavorful South Indian specialty that combines perfectly cooked basmati rice with tender marinated meat or vegetables, infused with a symphony of spices like cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, and star anise. Slow-cooked to perfection, every spoonful bursts with rich aromas and layers of taste, complemented by caramelized onions, fresh herbs, and a hint of saffron. Served with cooling raita and crisp papadum, our biryani is a celebration of tradition and culinary artistry that will delight your senses.

The Story of Dwaraka

Dwaraka South Indian Restaurant began with a simple but heartfelt vision — to bring the warmth, richness, and authenticity of South Indian cuisine to everyone, no matter where they come from. Inspired by the flavors of home and guided by traditional recipes passed down through generations, Dwaraka was born out of a passion for real food made with love. Our mission is to create a space where every dosa, every spoon of sambar, and every sip of filter coffee feels like a journey to the South — comforting, flavorful, and unforgettable. At Dwaraka, we don't just serve food — we share culture, tradition, and a sense of belonging.
